Introduction

Hello! Welcome to Styleguide - a gulp flavored living styleguide tool inspired by Huge's styleguide - but without the caveats and easily extendable.

Includes Browserify, sourcemaps, sass minification/concatenation, auto bower setup and concatenation of files, browser-sync, gh-pages and more.

Please feel free to collaborate to the project on Github. You can read the documentation here.
