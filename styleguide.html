Introduction

Hello! Welcome to Styleguide - a gulp flavored living styleguide tool inspired by Huge's styleguide - utilizing jade & json on top of an easily extendable. Includes Browserify, sourcemaps, sass minification/concatenation, auto bower setup and concatenation of files, browser-sync, gh-pages and more.

Please feel free to collaborate to the project on Github. You can submit any issues here.
